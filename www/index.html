San Bernardino County Superintendent of Schools (SBCSS) acts as an intermediate service agency between the state and local school districts to help meet the educational needs of all children countywide. Besides the main office in downtown San Bernardino, SBCSS has business offices in Colton and northern San Bernardino, plus service centers in Rancho Cucamonga and Apple Valley

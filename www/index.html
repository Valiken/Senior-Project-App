About SBCSS Fingertip Facts App

The SBCSS Fingertip Facts brochure provides an introduction to the county Superintendent of Schools, has countywide statistics relevant to education (enrollment numbers, for instance) and contact information for the organization and K-14 school districts throughout the county.

Our goal for this app is to provide an interactive, up-to-date mobile version of the San Bernardino County Superintendent of Schools' Fingertip Facts brochure. Whit this app we hope to leverage the latest technology providing not only up-to-date information, but quick access to phone numbers, email and driving directions for our staff, stakeholders and community partners.
